Introduction

YouTube (the world-famous video sharing website) maintains a list of the top trending videos, which indicates the audience interest across countries and the changing preferences over time.

This project explores the trending Youtube vidoes from Jan. 1, 2017 to May 31, 2018 in 9 countries (United States, Canada, Mexico, Great Britain, Germany, France, South Korea, Japan and India).
